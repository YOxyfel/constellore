Dev Logs / Updates

Follow Constellore from first light to its destination-first 3.0 universe.

  1. VERSION 3.0.0-BETA.2JUL 23, 2026LATEST

    The Path Becomes the Game

    A calmer First Orbit now teaches one visible destination before the rest of the universe unfolds. World Graph 3 adds a faster sparse runtime, hundreds of reviewed logical connections, ninety Daily challenge variants, one honest Guidance ladder, a live constellation trail, clearer Pure/Open/Practice/Study rules, privacy-safe missing-pair feedback, stronger ranked-run integrity, and the beta.2 compatibility fix for the Living Atlas.

    Pages and itch are deterministic local practice without live rankings, accounts, or AI. Beta progress may reset.

  2. VERSION 2.1.0-BETA.1JUL 22, 2026

    Signature Constellations

    Completed routes now earn a Signature grade for efficiency, discovery, originality, and fair play. The Living Atlas draws every performed recipe as a real star map; connected Voyages offer curated destination chains; successful recipes explain their logic; failed pairs can provide spoiler-safe directional clues; verified completions gain asynchronous community comparisons; and weekly Cosmic Events add themed targets and collectible discoveries without changing canonical recipes.

    Pages and itch are deterministic local practice without live rankings, accounts, or AI. Beta progress may reset.

  3. VERSION 2.0.0-BETA.1JUL 22, 2026

    The Living Atlas

    The catalog now holds 770 authored connections; 679 are naturally reachable from the four starters, producing 600+ distinct reachable results and 110+ same-word evolutions. Every mission starts with a clear target brief; help uses visible Open score rates; and new mastery, badges, momentum, diagnostics, accessibility, and release safeguards make every orbit easier to understand. Known beta limit: Pages and itch are deterministic local practice without live rankings, accounts, or AI. Beta progress may reset.

  4. UPDATE 03JUL 21, 2026

    Route Signals & Smoother Stamping

    Route Signals now guide you toward the next valid connection without revealing the answer or costing score. Hold SHIFT before or during a drag—even straight from Your universe—to stamp evenly spaced copies. Board messages now queue in one clear lane, so recipes, mastery, and control feedback no longer overlap.

  5. UPDATE 02JUL 21, 2026

    Shift Board Tools

    SHIFT over a board word clears it when your hand is empty. Hold Shift before or during any drag to leave evenly spaced copies; drop the held word onto another to fuse.

  6. UPDATE 01JUL 18, 2026

    Ctrl Hover Fusion

    Hold CTRL, hover one board word, then sweep onto another to combine instantly. The last result stays ready, making long discovery chains flow without extra clicks.

    Shipped with the release and recorded separately as the first gameplay update.
  7. PUBLIC BETA RELEASEJUL 16, 2026

    First Light

    Constellore opened its public browser beta: begin with four elements, build a constellation of discoveries, and find the target word across Reach, Quick Orbit, Move Limit, and daily play.
