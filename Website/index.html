WHY CONSTELLORE

Word crafting
with a purpose.

Open-ended combination games ask what you can make. Constellore also asks whether you can reach a specific destination. Your route—not only the final word—is the puzzle.

The current public beta is deterministic. Its official combinations come from a reviewed recipe map, so ranked answers are not invented by live AI.

QUICK ANSWERS

Before you
start crafting.

What am I trying to do?+

Create the target word shown for your run. Combine two existing words to discover a new one, then use those discoveries as steps toward the target.

Is the beta free?+

Yes. The public browser beta has no checkout or rewarded advertising. No account is required.

Does AI invent the answers?+

No. This public beta uses a prebuilt, reviewed recipe map. Experimental generation is not required for any official target and is not part of the local practice build.

Where is my progress saved?+

The public web and itch practice editions save progress in this browser. Clearing site data resets it, and this build does not upload gameplay analytics or scores.
