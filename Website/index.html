08 / QUESTIONS

Before you
enter orbit.

Is the beta free?＋

Yes. The current public beta has no live checkout or rewarded advertising. It is for testing the game, balance, and combination quality.

Do I need an account or download?＋

No. The browser creates a temporary guest identity on this device. A recoverable account system will arrive before paid ownership is enabled.

Can beta progress be reset?＋

Yes. Guest progress and leaderboards may be reset while the production account and database systems are being built.

Will every random combination work?＋

No, but the game aims for broad logical coverage rather than arbitrary rejection. Official targets always have a verified route.

Is the launch wishlist a store preorder?＋

No. It is an early interest signal, not a purchase or a Steam, Epic, Google Play, or App Store wishlist. The static preview labels GitHub stars separately; the online server can count one reversible anonymous browser signal.
